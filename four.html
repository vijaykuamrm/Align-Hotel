Protocol

How It Works

— — —

From the moment you dial, our precision response system ensures the right team reaches you in the shortest possible time.

01 📞
You Call

Dial the dedicated line for your emergency type, or 112 for central dispatch. A specialist answers within seconds.

02 🎯
Assessed

Our trained dispatcher assesses your situation, confirms your location, and determines the optimal response team.

03 🚁
Dispatched

The nearest qualified unit is immediately deployed. You receive live updates on arrival time via SMS.

04 ✅
Resolved

Elite professionals arrive, secure the scene and resolve the emergency. A full incident report is delivered to you.
